Overview:
This dataset comprises detailed transcripts of customer service interactions between clients and agents at Fresh Fare Meal Kits. Each entry captures a full exchange about various customer concerns ranging from delivery issues to subscription cancellations. The data is crucial for understanding customer service dynamics, identifying common issues, and improving service delivery based on real customer feedback.

Dataset Structure:
Logs: This is the primary column in the dataset, containing the full text of each customer-agent interaction. Each log entry includes timestamps, agent-client dialogues, and the specifics of the client's issue or request.

Each log entry is structured as follows:
Date
Time
Conversation Content
